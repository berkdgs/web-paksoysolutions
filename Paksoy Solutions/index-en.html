About Us

Paksoy Çözüm ve Danışmanlık was established in 2019 in order to increase the efficiency and competitiveness of companies in all national and international markets, and to improve efficiency by applying technology and innovations. For this purpose, it continues its activities in the field of audit, evaluation and consultancy with its experienced and knowledgeable team.

Our Policy

Paksoy Çözüm ve Danışmanlık A.Ş., based on its corporate vision and mission values, is to ensure the satisfaction of our customers with the most effective solutions in line with the needs and requests of its customers.
